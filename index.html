FMU Nature Inspired Computing Laboratory

The objective of this Laboratory is to develop eco-friendly computational tools inspired through Nature for solving societal problems.

Nature Inspired Computing Paradigms Used by this Laboratory:
  • Artificial Neural Networks
  • Genetic Algorithms
  • Particle Swarm Optimization
  • Artificial Bee Colony
  • Ant Colony Optimization
  • Biogeography Based Optimization
  • Chemical Reactions Based Optimization
  • Teaching Learning Based Optimization

This Laboratory is open for academic collaboration with the active members of groups of similar research interest.
